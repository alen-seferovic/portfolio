About Me

I am a senior at Purdue University studying Aeronautical & Astronautical Engineering, graduating in May 2026, with a focus on aerodynamics and coursework in structures. I am passionate about commercial aircraft and the critical engineering challenges involved in their development, including designing structures to withstand cyclic loading and performing rigorous aerodynamic testing to validate performance and efficiency. I also serve as an undergraduate teaching assistant for a fluid mechanics course (AAE 333), providing instructional support and mentoring students in engineering fundamentals.
